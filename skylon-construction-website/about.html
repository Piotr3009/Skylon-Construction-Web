About Skylon Construction

Skylon Construction delivers high-end construction, refurbishment and fit-out across London. We work on complex residential and commercial projects where the brief calls for precise coordination, a considered approach to existing buildings and a refined standard of finish throughout.

Our model is straightforward: one accountable team covering building works, bespoke joinery, electrical coordination and architectural detailing. Keeping these disciplines aligned under a single point of responsibility means fewer gaps between trades, clearer communication and a result that reflects the original intent of the design.

Part of the wider Skylon group

Skylon Construction is part of the wider Skylon group, a small family of specialist businesses that share the same name, standards and approach to quality. The group also includes Prime Sash Windows, which focuses on traditional and timber window work.

Being part of that group gives us a consistent way of working and access to related specialist knowledge when a project calls for it. Each business keeps its own focus, so on a Skylon Construction project you deal with a construction team that is accountable for the build from start to finish.
