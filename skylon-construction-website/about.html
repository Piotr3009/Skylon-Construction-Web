Our story

From inspired vision to luxurious execution

What began as a family business has grown into a trusted construction company delivering luxury homes, complex refurbishments, extensions and commercial developments across London and Hertforshire.

By bringing the specialist trades together under one group, we provide a seamless experience from concept to completion, ensuring every project is delivered with precision, efficiency and exceptional attention to detail.

Today Skylon Construction leads projects from first survey to final handover, with the Group’s specialist companies working as one crew, on one programme, to one standard.
