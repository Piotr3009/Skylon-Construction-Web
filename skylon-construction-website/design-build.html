Design & Build

One team. One contract. One promise.

Architecture, costing and construction under a single point of responsibility — from the first sketch to the final coat of paint, across London and Hertfordshire.

Why design & build

The most expensive gap in construction is the one between the designer and the builder.

In a traditional project, the architect draws, the contractor prices, and every difference between the two becomes your problem — in variations, delays and disputes.

Design & Build closes that gap. One team carries your project from concept to completion, so buildability, cost and detail are resolved on paper — where changes are cheap — not on site, where they are not.

What it changes

Four problems D&B removes

One contract, one budget

A single agreement covering design and construction — no gaps between scopes for costs to hide in.

Costs known early

Because the builder is in the room from day one, the design develops against a real budget — itemised, with assumptions stated.

Designed to be built

Details are drawn by people who will make them — including our own joinery workshop and spray facility.

Faster overall

Design, pricing and preparation overlap instead of queueing — weeks saved before ground is even broken.

How it runs

From sketch to keys

  • 1. Consultation. Your site, your brief, your budget — honestly discussed.
  • 2. Design & feasibility. Concepts developed against real costs, planning strategy included.
  • 3. Fixed proposal. An itemised price and programme you can plan around.
  • 4. Construction. In-house trades, a named site manager, a written report every fortnight.
  • 5. Handover & aftercare. Certified, documented, snag-free — and we stay reachable.

Fair questions

Before you ask

We already have an architect — can you still help?

Yes. We regularly build from architects’ designs and can join at any stage — pricing, value-engineering and delivering the scheme while keeping your designer involved. Design & Build is one way we work, not the only way.

Is Design & Build more expensive?

Usually the opposite over the life of a project: costs are engineered into the design early, and variations — the classic budget killer — largely disappear. Our proposals are itemised, so you can see exactly what you are paying for.

Do we lose control of the design?

No — you gain a design partner whose drawings come with a price attached. Every significant decision is presented with options and costs before it is made, and nothing proceeds without your sign-off.
