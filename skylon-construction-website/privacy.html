Privacy Policy

How Skylon Construction Ltd collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: [date]

1. Who we are

Skylon Construction Ltd (“Skylon”, “we”, “us”) is a construction company registered in England & Wales, Company No. 09720017. Registered office: 31 Roe Hill Close, Hatfield, Hertfordshire AL10 9JE. Head office: Tyttenhanger House, Coursers Road, St Albans, Hertfordshire AL4 0PG. For any privacy matter, contact us at info@skylonconstruction.com.

2. Information we collect

When you contact us: through the enquiry form, by email or by phone. We collect the details you provide: your name, email address, phone number and any information about your project. We do not collect more than we need to respond to you and to deliver our services.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, prepare proposals, deliver contracted works, meet legal and contractual obligations, and keep reasonable business records. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party marketing.

4. Legal basis

We process personal data under UK GDPR on the following bases: your consent (when you submit an enquiry), performance of a contract (when we work together), and our legitimate interests in operating and protecting our business.

5. How long we keep it

Enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any follow-up. Project and contractual records are retained for the periods required by law, tax rules and construction warranty obligations, after which they are securely deleted.

6. Sharing

We share information only where necessary to deliver a project, for example with professional consultants, Skylon Group companies working on your project, or our IT and email providers, and only under appropriate safeguards. We may disclose information where required by law.

7. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct or erase your personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email info@skylonconstruction.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

This website uses only the technical storage necessary for it to function.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the date of the most recent revision shown above.
