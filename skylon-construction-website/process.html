Process · London

A controlled construction process from first conversation to handover.

Every Skylon project follows a clear, coordinated path. From the first consultation through survey, costing and build to handover and aftercare, you always know the current stage, the next step and what you can expect to receive.

How we work

Eight stages, fully coordinated

One integrated team manages each stage in sequence, so decisions, costs and quality stay transparent from start to finish.

01

Initial Consultation

We discuss your project, priorities, constraints and budget context to understand what you want to achieve and how we can help.

You receive: a written summary of the conversation and our initial view on the most practical next step.

02

Drawings / Site Review

We review any existing drawings and arrange a site visit to assess the property, access and the current condition of the structure and services.

You receive: a site review note setting out key observations, constraints and any information still required.

03

Scope Clarification

We define a clear, realistic scope of works together, confirming what is included, what is excluded and where decisions are still open.

You receive: a documented scope of works that forms the basis for costing and programme.

04

Budget & Programme

We prepare a structured cost breakdown and a realistic programme so you can plan around clear figures and timelines before committing.

You receive: an itemised cost breakdown and an indicative project programme.

05

Pre-Construction Planning

We finalise specifications, coordinate trades and services, confirm lead times and prepare the logistics and access arrangements for the build.

You receive: a confirmed start date, a delivery schedule and a single point of contact for the project.

06

Construction Phase

We deliver the works under tight site management, coordinating construction, joinery and services while keeping the property protected and tidy.

You receive: regular progress updates and a clear route for questions and decisions throughout the build.

07

Quality Control

We carry out structured checks and a detailed snagging review against the agreed specification before anything is considered complete.

You receive: a snagging list with items resolved and signed off ahead of handover.

08

Handover & Aftercare

We hand over the finished project, walk you through the completed works and remain available to support you once you are settled in.

You receive: a handover pack with relevant documentation, warranties and aftercare contact details.

Before we talk

What to send us before the first consultation

A few details ahead of our first conversation help us give you a more useful, focused response. Share whatever you have to hand.

  • Drawings, if available
  • Photos of the property or space
  • Address / location
  • Approximate budget, if known
  • Desired timeline
  • Planning status
  • Any structural / M&E information
If you do not have drawings yet, Skylon can still advise the next step.
