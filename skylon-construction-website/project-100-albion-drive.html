The brief

Collaboration from Concept to Completion.

Working in close collaborstion with the cleint's architect, we delivered a complete refurbishment that carefully balanced the design intent with the practical requirements of the building. Regular coordination and open communication throughout the project ensured every detail aligned with the client's expectations, allowing design decisions, programme requirements and the site activities to remain fully coordinated. This collaborative approach enabled us to deliver a high-quality finish that met the client's vision.

Scope of works

What we delivered 

  • Rear extention
  • Top floor extention 
  • New electrical, plumbing and ventilation services
  • Bespoke joinery items 

The challenges

Delivering Quality Starts with Quality People.

Finding skilled specialist trades is one of the biggest challanges in today's construction industry. Labour shortages and high demand can make it difficult to secure experienced professionals, creating risk to programme delivery and project quality.

Be relying on our trusted network of specialist subcontractors ad careful resourse planning, we secured the right people at the right time, keeping the project on a roll and delivering the required standard of workmanship without compromise.

The outcome

Ready for the Next Chapter

The project transformed the cleint's vision into a market-ready commercial space, combining high-quality finishes with fully integrated, functional building services. Beyond creating an attractive interior, every mechanical and electrical system was installed, tested, comissioned, and signed off to ensure the building was safe, compliant, and ready for immediate occupation. The completed refurbishment provided the client with a modern, fully operational asset, ready to welcome new tenants and maximise its appeal on the market.
