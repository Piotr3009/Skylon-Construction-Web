The brief

Premium Office Accomodation in the Heart of the City.

This project transformed a dated and uninspiring office into a contemporary, welcoming workspace, delivered in line with the architects vision. The refurbishment combined high-quality finishes with modern design features to create an attractive environment that enhances both, functionality and user experience.

Scope of works

What we delivered

  • New M&E services
  • Refurbishment throughout
  • Firestopping services
  • Bespoke joinery 

The challenges

The Difference is in the Detail...

A key challange of this project was restoring the reception's feature pendant lights, which had become dusty and lost their original appearance over time. Die to their high-level installation, large size and fragile construction, the process required careful planning and specialist access equipment.

Each pendant was safely removed, cleaned inside and out, and reinstalled by a team of operatives working together to prevent damage. The successful restoration preserved a striking design feature while enhancing the overall quality of the refurbished reception.

The outcome

Raising the Standards

Our scope included the complete fit-out of the office space, incorporating new architectural finishes, upgraded mechanical and electrical (M&E) services, and comprehensive firestopping works. The result is a modern, efficient workplace that meets current buuidling standards while providing an inviting and visually striking environment for its occupants.
