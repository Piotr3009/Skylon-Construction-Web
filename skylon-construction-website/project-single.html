Chelsea Townhouse Refurbishment

A full refurbishment of a period townhouse in Chelsea — reconfigured layout, renewed services, bespoke joinery and architectural finishing delivered by one integrated team.

Chelsea, London

Full Refurbishment

Project Overview

This Chelsea townhouse called for a complete refurbishment that would modernise how the home worked day to day while respecting its period character. Working from the existing structure outward, we reconfigured the internal layout, renewed the building services and reinstated a calm, considered material palette across every floor.

As a single integrated team, we coordinated construction, bespoke joinery, electrical works and final finishing under one programme. That continuity kept decisions clear, protected the level of detailing throughout and gave the client a single point of accountability from strip-out to handover.

Approach

Challenge, solution and result

Challenge

A constrained period property with a dated layout, tired services and limited natural light, all on a tight central London site with restricted access and a need to protect existing character.

Solution

A coordinated refurbishment plan that opened up key living spaces, renewed first and second fix services, and integrated bespoke joinery early so detailing was designed into the build rather than added afterwards.

Result

A refined, well-ordered home with improved flow, upgraded services and a consistent premium finish across every floor, delivered under tight quality control and handed over fully snagged.

Scope of Works

  • Strip-out and demolition
  • Structural alterations
  • New first and second fix
  • Bespoke joinery
  • Electrical coordination
  • Plastering and decoration
  • Bathrooms and kitchen
  • Snagging and handover
