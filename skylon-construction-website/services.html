What we deliver

Ten services, one accountable team

Each discipline is delivered and coordinated by the same team, so scope, programme and finish stay aligned from first fix to handover.

Residential Construction

Whole-home builds and structural works for prime London homes, delivered to a refined, consistent finish.

  • New builds and full structural construction
  • Load-bearing alterations and steelwork
  • Integrated services and first-fix coordination
  • Premium finishes through to completion

Ideal for: homeowners commissioning a complete, high-specification build.

Extensions

Single and multi-storey extensions that add space and light while sitting naturally with the existing property.

  • Rear, side-return and wrap-around extensions
  • Structural openings to connect old and new
  • Glazing, roofing and weather-tight build-up
  • Internal remodelling to suit the new layout

Ideal for: growing households extending a home they intend to keep.

Refurbishments

Complete renovations that modernise layout, services and finishes while protecting period character.

  • Full-property and floor-by-floor refurbishment
  • Reconfigured layouts and new services
  • Restoration of period features and detailing
  • Kitchens, bathrooms and joinery integrated

Ideal for: owners upgrading an existing home top to bottom.

Basement Works

Basement construction and conversions that create usable lower-ground space with careful structural control.

  • Excavation, underpinning and structural slabs
  • Waterproofing and tanking systems
  • Drainage, ventilation and services routing
  • Habitable finishes to living-space standard

Ideal for: homeowners adding space without extending the footprint.

Commercial Fit-Out

Workspace and commercial interiors built to programme, with services and finishes fully coordinated.

  • Category A and Category B fit-out
  • Partitions, ceilings and floor build-up
  • Mechanical and electrical coordination
  • Joinery, reception and feature areas

Ideal for: occupiers and landlords preparing commercial space.

Office Refurbishment

Refreshing existing workplaces with minimal disruption, improving layout, services and finish in occupied or vacant space.

  • Reconfigured layouts and meeting spaces
  • Lighting, power and data upgrades
  • Flooring, ceilings and acoustic treatment
  • Phased works around business operations

Ideal for: businesses modernising their current premises.

Bespoke Joinery Coordination

Cabinetry, panelling and made-to-measure joinery designed into the build, not bolted on at the end.

  • Fitted cabinetry, wardrobes and storage
  • Wall panelling and architectural detailing
  • Workshop-made elements to set tolerances
  • Site fit coordinated with services and finishes

Ideal for: projects where detailing and fit define the result.

Electrical Coordination

Electrical and services planned early to avoid clashes, rework and compromised finishes later on.

  • Power, lighting and data layout planning
  • First-fix and second-fix sequencing
  • Lighting design integration with finishes
  • Coordination with qualified electrical contractors

Ideal for: builds with considered lighting and services design.

Architectural Elements

Staircases, fireplaces and feature construction delivered as considered, well-detailed parts of the whole.

  • Feature staircases and balustrades
  • Fireplaces, surrounds and built-in features
  • Specialist plaster, stone and tiling
  • Detailing coordinated with structure and services

Ideal for: clients investing in standout architectural detail.

Project Management

A single point of contact, defined scope and clear reporting that keep decisions, programme and costs transparent.

  • Programme planning and trade sequencing
  • Cost tracking against agreed scope
  • Regular updates and decision points
  • Snagging, sign-off and handover

Ideal for: clients who want one team accountable end to end.
